Identity theft

Cybercriminals require information like your name, date of birth, and Social Security number to commit identity theft. Many data brokers compile the exact data points these criminals seek.

Increased costs

Financial and risk assessment data brokers sell information such as browsing histories and credit scores to entities like insurance companies and banks. This can result in higher insurance premiums and loan rejections, among other financial consequences.
